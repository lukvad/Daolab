Seaman DID

The Seaman DID (Decentralized Identifier) ​​project, based on blockchain technology (technology scattered registry) and cryptographic functions, would allow the owner of his seafarer documents to fully control, secure digital storage and remote confirmation of your professional profile in the case of e.g. applying for position with the shipowner or confirmation of their qualifications. The current development of the blockchain ecosystem in the use of DID gives us the ability to protect ourselves against forgery of certificates, which are extremely important in the shipping industry sea ​​with from the point of view of, above all, the safety of human life, but also the requirements of the shipowner and authorities administration sea. Full digitization of the seafarer's profile opens up many surprising possibilities and informatization flow automation data for this industry. A distributed system in digital form built of many trusted equals units (e.g. offices, training centers) as in the case of the most popular equivalent of the financial industry - Bitcoin, ensures that data cannot be tampered with, intercepted, and the network service cannot be attacked.

App and IoT system for YUMI.ECO

The YUMI.ECO application works in the Tri-City area. For a fee of PLN 0.49 per minute of driving a vehicle, residents are supplied with a fleet of 40 electric scooters parked after passing through the zone set by the server application. The realtime database supports instant payment Tpay and PayPal, among others payment cards. Employees have an administration app available to oversee the fleet and keep the batteries charged. Mobile apps are available on Google Play and the App Store. Electronics program the on-board design of the scooters, along with the designs of PCBs and subassemblies. We served approx. 3000 regular users. Currently, the system design and fleet has been resold.
